Research Projects

Artificial Curiosity

Project in AI and Goal Exploration

Intrinsically motivated goal exploration algorithms for autotelic agents. We are creating AI agents, giving them an environment, and letting them create their own goals. The agent learns about the environment through its own curiosity. Then we analyze its internal structures to find out how "conscious" it is (consciousness/understanding is very hard to quantify, we are looking for meaningful representations).

Fine Tuning LLMs to Emulate Instructor

Project in Intelligent Tutoring Systems

TIC-TOC: Enhancing Industrial Control Education through an Intelligent Tutoring System for Real-Time Interaction and Engagement. Researching the best intelligent tutoring systems and combining them with fine-tuned multi-modal LLMs to create a novel learning experience that emulates individual professor's feedback for the engineering students at TAMU. We already have permission to implement the ITS in a TAMU materials class for the Spring 2025 semester.
