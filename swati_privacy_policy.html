Swati AI Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Swati AI Chrome Extension

Swati AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Data Collection and Usage

Swati AI does not collect or store any personal information. The extension solely functions to provide simplified explanations for highlighted text using a secure external API. No data is retained or analyzed beyond what is necessary for this functionality.

Permissions

Swati AI requires limited permissions to operate:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the active tab where the user highlights text.
  • storage: Used to save user preferences related to the extension.

These permissions are strictly used for the core functionality of the extension and do not involve any form of tracking or data analysis.

External API

Swati AI utilizes the Google Gemini API to generate text explanations. No user-identifiable information is sent to this API, ensuring user privacy.
